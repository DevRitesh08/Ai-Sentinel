# Launch Kit

This file is the playbook for making AI Trust Sentinel visible once the public demo is live.

## Core Positioning

### One-line pitch

AI Trust Sentinel is an open-source trust layer for AI answers that turns a raw response into a scored, sourced, sentence-level verification report.

### Two-line pitch

Most AI apps stop at generation. AI Trust Sentinel adds a second layer: claim extraction, live web checks, consistency signals, and a 0-100 trust score so users can see what to trust.

### Good audience targets

- AI engineers building agent or chatbot products
- Product teams who need safer AI UX
- Hackathon judges looking for practical AI infra
- Developers interested in hallucination detection and evals
